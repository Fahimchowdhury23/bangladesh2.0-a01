Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"


Innovation

The process of bringing about new
ideas, that have a significant
positive impact and value


Sustainability

The idea of using resources in a
way that can be maintained over
time, without depleting them.


Infrastructure

The organizational structures
and basic physical for the
operation of a society.


Empowerment

Empowerment is the degree of
autonomy and self-determination
in people and in communities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in
renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.


Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to
enhance connectivity and access to services for
all citizens.


Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure
a sustainable and eco-friendly future.


Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support
economic growth and global competitiveness.

spend time discussing issues of poverty and hunger. They believe that these will be resolved when general economic prosperity increases. These economists spend
all their talents detailing the process of development and prosperity, but rarely reflect on the origin and development of poverty and hunger. As a result, poverty continues."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
